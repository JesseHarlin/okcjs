First Meeting: January 2012

Jan 3rd, 2012

The inagural OKC.js meeting will be in January, and the exact time and details are yet to be determined. All we know for sure right now is that it will be hosted at the okcCoCo in Midtown OKC.

If you would like to propose a talk for the meeting or volunteer to speak, please email vance@brightb.it with your ideas.
